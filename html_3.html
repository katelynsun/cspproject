The Beatles

The Beatles were an English rock band consisting of four teenagers from Liverpool. The group performed at small venues in 1960 and 1961. When Brian Epstein saw the group play at the Cavern Club, he was curious after hearing mention of the band. He returned to take in a few more shows and on December 10, 1961, Epstein approached the band and a five-year contract was signed in January of 1962.
