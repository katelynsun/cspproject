Explore the popular artist for each genre.

Rock

During the 40s, the popular genres of blues and country music were combined using powerful electric guitar riffs and drums to create a more "aggressive" genre than the originals. In the 50s, Chuck Berry, a pioneer of rock music, grew in popularity, which led to the growth of rock music as a whole. People appreciated the aspect of defiance that rock music popularized, both to popular music of the past and to the conservative society of the time.
